What makes Govori different

🔍

Search Any Form

Type the word as it appears in a text — even inflected. The app finds the dictionary entry and shows you the full picture.

Searching "reci" resolves to "reći" (verb).

🔤

Latin and Cyrillic, Your Choice

Switch between scripts at any time. Search in either. The app handles diacritics and diacritic-free variants equally.

"zena" = "žena" = "жена"

📖

Declension & Conjugation Tables

Not just definitions — full morphology. See every noun case, every verb tense, adjective gender/number/definiteness.

7 cases for nouns. Imperfective and perfective verb aspects.

🌍

English + Russian Meanings

Each entry includes English glosses and Russian translations, with usage examples in both languages.

⚡

Offline, Fast, Private

No network required. No account. No tracking. The entire database fits in 6 MB on your device.

Lookup speed: <5ms for exact matches.
