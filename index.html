See what Govori can do

Find it your way

Search in Latin or Cyrillic, with or without diacritics. Look up any word form — even conjugated verbs or declined nouns — or search by English or Russian translation. Govori figures out what you mean.

Understand the full picture

Interactive tables show every case, tense, and number at once. Each entry highlights accent, gender, transitivity, register, and dialect — plus real usage examples in Serbian, English, and Russian.

Make it yours

Your recent lookups and favorites are always one tap away. Switch between Latin and Cyrillic anytime — the entire app switches instantly.

Who it's for

Language learners

Quickly check an accent, look up a tricky case ending, or explore verb forms mid-lesson. A handy companion whether you're hanging around in Belgrade or doing your homework.

Heritage speakers

You grew up hearing Serbian but never studied the grammar formally. Finally make sense of all those endings.

Translators and linguists

Quick, structured morphological reference with regional variants. Every form indexed and searchable, plus quick lookup in Wiktionary, Hrvatski jezični portal, and Google.
