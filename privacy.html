Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

What we collect

When you sign up for the beta, we collect:

  • Your email address (required)
  • How you're learning Serbian (optional)

The Govori app requires no account and works fully offline. To improve stability, it collects anonymous crash reports and performance diagnostics via Firebase Crashlytics. This data is not linked to your identity and is used solely to fix bugs and improve app performance.

How we use it

Your email is used to send you a TestFlight beta invite and to notify you when the app launches on the App Store. That's it.

Who has access

Only the developer (that's me) and Formspree, which processes form submissions. Your email is not sold, shared, or used for advertising.

Retention

Your data is kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion. To delete your data, email support@govoridictionaries.com.

Cookies & tracking

This website uses no cookies, no analytics, and no tracking scripts.

Contact

Questions? Email support@govoridictionaries.com.
